🛍️ OVERVIEW OF EACH SHOPPING APP ALONG WITH THEIR FOCUS MATERIALS OR PRODUCT CATEGORIES 🔍

🛒 AMAZON

OVERVIEW: Amazon is a global e-commerce leader, providing an extensive selection of products across categories like electronics, clothing, books, and groceries. It is known for its comprehensive shopping experience and fast delivery options. 🚀
PRODUCT CATEGORIES: 📱 Electronics, 👗 Fashion, 🏠 Home Goods, 📚 Books, 🛒 Groceries, 💄 Beauty Products.

📦 FLIPKART

OVERVIEW: Flipkart is one of India's largest online marketplaces, focusing on a diverse range of products, particularly electronics and fashion. It has played a significant role in popularizing online shopping within India. 🇮🇳
PRODUCT CATEGORIES:📱 Electronics, 👕 Fashion, 🏠 Home Appliances, 🛋️ Furniture, 👶 Baby Products.

📲 MEESHO

OVERVIEW: Meesho is a social commerce platform in India that enables individuals, especially women, to start their own online businesses by reselling products through social media. It aims to empower entrepreneurs with zero investment. 💼
PRODUCT CATEGORIES:👗 Clothing, 💍 Accessories, 🏡 Home Décor, 💅 Beauty Products.

👗 MYNTRA

OVERVIEW: Myntra specializes in fashion and lifestyle products in India, offering a wide range of apparel and accessories from both international and local brands. It’s a go-to platform for fashion enthusiasts. ✨
PRODUCT CATEGORIES:👕 Clothing, 👟 Footwear, 🎀 Accessories, 💄 Beauty Products, 🛋️ Lifestyle Items.

🛍️ SHOPSY

OVERVIEW: Shopsy, an initiative by Flipkart, is tailored for small sellers and businesses in India, enabling them to reach customers with affordable products across various categories. 💰
PRODUCT CATEGORIES:👗 Clothing, 📱 Electronics, 🏠 Home Essentials, 💄 Beauty Products, and more.
