Meesho is a social commerce platform that allows small businesses and resellers to sell products through WhatsApp, Facebook, and Instagram. 💬📤 Founded in 2015, it is known for its affordable fashion, home decor, and beauty products. 👚🏡💄 With a zero-commission model, Meesho is a great platform for entrepreneurs looking to start an online business with minimal investment. 🚀 Backed by SoftBank and Facebook, it continues to grow rapidly in the Indian market. 📈✨
